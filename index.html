Events

EPoD-GL Lunch
Monthly. This will be a hands-on technical workshop with our neighbors upstairs, The Growth Lab. Each month, alternating, we present a paper from our field (1 hour) and then do a practical exercise together (1 hour). Practical exercises might include: replicating analyses, creating data visualizations or maps, and so on. We'll replicate analyses, such as x = y.
F(AF)T
Monthly. Fellows (and Friends) Tea. Come one, come all! A randomista get-together, for people working across our organizations: J-PAL, IPA, EPoD, HKS, HBS, other acronyms. More informal. More casual. More manatees. We'll learn stuff like what is a for loop in Python look like, or what the <em> is. We'll also learn about the efficiency of CTRL+C and other keyboard shortcuts.
