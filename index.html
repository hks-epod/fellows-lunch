Hi. This is going to be the website for the EPoD Research Fellows (and Friends) Lunch. Check back soon - and you'll see more stuff than just a Calming Manatee.

We're on GitHub! Interested in tinkering with this site? Clone the files to your computer and get coding! Open Terminal (OSX) or PowerShell (Windows), navigate to wherever you want to put it (cd [some file path]) and type:

git clone http://github.com/hks-epod/fellows-lunch

Voila.

Events

For the full calendar, click here.
EPoD/Growth Lab Lunch monthly This will be a hands-on technical workshop with our neighbors upstairs, The Growth Lab. Each month, alternating, we present a paper from our field (1 hour) and then do a practical exercise together (1 hour). Practical exercises might include: replicating analyses, creating data visualizations or maps, and so on. We'll replicate analyses, such as x = y.
F(AF)T monthly Fellows (and Friends) Tea. Come one, come all! A randomista get-together, for people working across our organizations: J-PAL, IPA, EPoD, HKS, HBS, other acronyms. More informal. More casual. More manatees. We'll learn stuff like what is a for loop in Python look like, or what the <em> is. We'll also learn about the efficiency of CTRL+C and other keyboard shortcuts.
